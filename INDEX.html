Tardis

What is Tardis?


Tardis or Time and Relative Dimensions in Space is a hybrid of a Time Machine and a spacecraft.Its exterior appearance mimics a police box, an obsolete type of telephone kiosk that was once commonly seen on streets in Britain.TARDISes are built with a "chameleon circuit", a type of camouflage technology that changes the exterior form of the ship to blend into the environment of whatever time or place it lands in.While the exterior is of limited size, the TARDIS is famously "bigger on the inside". Behind the police box doors lies a large control room, at the centre of which is a hexagonal console for operating the TARDIS. In the middle of the console is a moving tubular device called a time rotor. The presence of a physically larger space contained within the police box is explained as "dimensionally transcendental", with the interior being a whole separate dimension containing an infinite number of rooms, corridors and storage spaces, all of which can change their appearance and configuration but now, the tardis is controlled by this website.


What's New in this version?


This is a much more high tech version of Tardis where in this version, you can control everything from your computer. You can also Journal about your experiences while travelling through space and time. A Historical Database has been built so you can see where you last time travelled or travelled in space. Through the console, you can control the chameleon circuit and decide where you want to time travel or travel through space. Emergency Protocols are also listed so if the tardis malfunctions, please follow the listed protocols to be safe. There is also a log of your resources from which you can see the available resources in the tardis. To access tardis you need to make an account and register yourself. Then, you need to log-in to access tardis. After logging in, you can access all of tardis's feautures.
